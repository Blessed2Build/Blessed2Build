About Us

Blessed 2 Build is dedicated to helping families who have lost everything due to war and political instability. We support them in rebuilding their lives and advocating for peace.

Our Mission

Our mission is to assist displaced families facing extreme challenges by providing resources, support, and advocacy to help them rebuild their lives and regain stability.

Our Vision

We envision a peaceful, blessed world free from hunger, where mental stability and comfort enable families to grow and students to focus on their education while advocating for peace in war zones.

Our Team

Founder: Wegene Bezaye

I started Blessed 2 Build because my family and I have personally experienced the devastating effects of political instability and displacement. I am originally from Ethiopia and came to the USA to study at Clark Atlanta University. Just a month after arriving, I learned that my family's home had been demolished due to the ongoing political instability. They were forced to leave the city they had lived in and relocate to a small town in the countryside.
